Such transition and physical risk events driven by climate change can have broad impact to our customers, operations, suppliers and business. Climate-related risk is interconnected and overarching across all risk types as it may manifest as credit risk, operational risk, market risk, liquidity risk and other risk types. We are currently performing a risk identification process for climate-related risk to determine the meaningfulness and measurability of the risk.
