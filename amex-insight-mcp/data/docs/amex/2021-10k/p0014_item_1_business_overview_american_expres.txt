The American Express Brand and Service Excellence Our brand and its attributes trust, security and service are key assets. We invest heavily in managing, marketing, promoting and protecting our brand, including through the delivery of our products and services in a manner consistent with our brand promise. The American Express brand is ranked among the most valuable brands in the world. We place significant importance on trademarks, service marks and patents, and seek to secure our intellectual property rights around the world. We aim to provide the world s best customer experience every day and our reputation for world-class service has been recognized by numerous awards over the years.
